## What is Mounjaro Info?

**Mounjaro Info** is an independent information website dedicated to **Mounjaro (tirzepatide)**, a medication developed by Eli Lilly. Our mission: making medical and scientific information about tirzepatide accessible in French, English and Traditional Chinese.

## Tirzepatide at a glance

Tirzepatide is a **dual GIP and GLP-1 receptor agonist**, a new class of medication that acts on two hormonal pathways to:

- Improve glycemic control in type 2 diabetes patients
- Promote significant weight loss
- Reduce cardiovascular risk factors

### Regulatory approvals

- **FDA** (United States): approved May 2022 for type 2 diabetes
- **EMA** (Europe): approved September 2023
- **Zepbound**: weight management formulation (FDA, November 2023)
