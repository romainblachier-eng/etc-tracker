## A clear vocation

France Formosa is a Franco-Taiwanese cultural diplomacy association. Its mission is to strengthen the cultural and diplomatic ties between France and Taiwan, bridging academic output, institutional relations and concrete cultural projects.

Built on the conviction that relations between two democracies are also forged through culture, research and civic dialogue, France Formosa serves as a bridge between French and Taiwanese civil societies.

## Our founding principles

### Intellectual independence
Our analyses and publications are guided by academic rigor and objectivity, free from partisan affiliation.

### Intercultural dialogue
We believe in the richness of exchanges between cultures and civilizations, and in the power of dialogue to bring peoples closer together.

### Democratic commitment
France and Taiwan share fundamental democratic values that we strive to promote and defend.

### Concrete action
Beyond reflection, we carry out field actions: diplomatic missions, conferences, cultural events and institutional partnerships.
