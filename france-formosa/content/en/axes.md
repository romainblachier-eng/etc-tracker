## Intellectual and academic production

France Formosa contributes to public debate through publications in leading journals such as **Revue Conflits**, the **Taipei Times** and **Taiwan Insight**. Our members regularly participate in higher education institutions and conferences dedicated to Taiwanese geopolitics.

Our ambition is to foster quality reflection on Franco-Taiwanese issues and to promote French expertise on Taiwan.

---

## Field diplomacy

The association has conducted **two official missions under the auspices of Taiwan's MOFA** (Ministry of Foreign Affairs) in **2019** and **2025**, demonstrating the institutional recognition of our work.

We maintain ongoing relations with:
- The **Taipei Representative Office in France**
- The **Taiwan Office in Aix-en-Provence**
- **MOFA Foreign Media Services**

These connections allow us to act as a facilitator in bilateral exchanges.

---

## Cultural mediation and events

France Formosa develops conference projects, event partnerships and initiatives aimed at **strengthening Taiwan's visibility in France** and encouraging intercultural exchanges.

We organize and participate in events that highlight Taiwan's cultural richness for the French public.

In 2024, France Formosa organized a **Taiwan symposium at the French National Assembly**, bringing together French parliamentarians and experts on Franco-Taiwanese relations.

---

## Institutional partnerships development

We have initiated an approach with **Taiwan's Ministry of Culture (MOC)** to structure lasting partnerships in cultural mediation, events and exchanges between the two countries.

This approach reflects our commitment to building solid institutional bridges between France and Taiwan.
